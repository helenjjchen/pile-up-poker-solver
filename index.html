What counts as a scoring way?

The solver is trying to maximize dollars first, then preserve truly different scoring structures for tied outcomes. It folds rotations/reflections of the same board, suit-only swaps when suits do not affect the hand, and irrelevant kickers for pairs, trips, quads, and two pair.

It still shows separate ways when the same total, hand count, and quality count come from different hand type mixes, such as one placement using trips plus two pair while another uses a pair plus a straight. Those representatives are saved for future runs. Counts say “ways found” because proving the best score does not by itself prove that every tied scoring way has been enumerated.
